# Interview Notes

## Why use fact and dimension tables?
To separate measurable events from descriptive attributes and support efficient analytics.

## Why is pay_period a dimension?
It allows consistent time-based analysis and supports payroll calendar changes.

## How would you handle retro pay?
By inserting adjustment records linked to the original pay period but processed in a later one.

## How do you audit payroll?
By reconciling gross to net, validating deductions and taxes, and flagging variances without modifying history.
